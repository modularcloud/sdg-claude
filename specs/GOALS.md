GOALS.md is a tripwire against drift, not a spec summary. A goal may be added only when it clears **all three bars** — **significant** (the product is a failure without it), **relevant** (Developer demonstrably cares about it), and **durable** (unlikely to change — and if it ever did, that would be a big deal deserving a deliberate decision, which is exactly the friction the explicit-approval rule provides) — and only with Developer's explicit approval, obtained through the process. AI actors suggest goals sparingly, when something crossing all three bars emerges from the work; Developer may add or suggest goals at any time. Keep it under 1000 words, bullet-pointed, and skeptical of every addition.
